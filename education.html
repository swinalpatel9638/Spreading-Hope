EDUCATION IS THE DIFFERENCE

Education is the difference for millions of children around the globe. Whether trapped in poverty or stuck in exploitation, education offers hope of a better future. We've made it our mission to connect all children with their right to learn. Support our Education Programme today.

WHAT WE DO

Currently, we operate 155 schools in Northern Syria, making us the largest non-governmental education provider in the country. Our commitment extends beyond the present generation; we aspire to open doors of opportunity for Syrian children today and for generations to come.

In September 2023, we achieved a significant milestone by successfully building a new secondary school in an area of Northwestern Syria where many children, including 11-year-old Zayna, previously lacked access to secondary education.

Maria's STORY

"I am absolutely thrilled to begin my first day in secondary school next week. My mum and dad were worried about what I'd do after finishing primary school last year. We are so happy that there is now a new school in the camp and I can't wait to see my friends and start learning again!"

The establishment of the secondary school marks just the beginning. With additional funding, we can further enhance our impact by employing more teaching staff and expanding our classrooms. Your contributions have made it possible for these children to access quality education, a fundamental right that will undoubtedly shape their future and empower them to reach their full potential.

Education Programs

Our program is dedicated to bringing hope and stability to displaced Syrian families affected by the ongoing conflict. By addressing immediate needs and fostering long-term recovery, we aim to alleviate suffering and empower communities.

Our efforts span from providing essential aid like food and shelter to promoting economic growth and social development. With a focus on rebuilding lives and nurturing resilience, we strive to create a brighter, sustainable future for those who have endured unimaginable hardships, helping them reclaim their lives with dignity.

How You Can Help

Spreading Hope is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering Syrians to rebuild their lives and their communities through sustainable life-changing programs and services. Our comprehensive strategy includes education, training, job placement, economic empowerment, and research studies.

We also prioritize women empowerment and child protection, working to ensure that all Syrians have the support and resources they need to thrive and succeed.
