How You Can Help

Spreading Hope is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering Syrians to rebuild their lives and their communities through sustainable life-changing programs and services. Our comprehensive strategy includes education, training, job placement, economic empowerment, and research studies.

We also prioritize women empowerment and child protection, working to ensure that all Syrians have the support and resources they need to thrive and succeed.

About Us

Spreading hope is a non-profit organization constantly thriving to improve the lives of Syrians. Syrians have been victims of this dreadful civil war for the last 12 years. A peaceful uprising against the president of Syria 12 years ago turned into a full-scale civil war. The conflict has left half a million people dead, devastated cities, and drawn in other countries. This NGO was started to help Syrians by collecting funds and allocating them to housing, food, health, and education.
